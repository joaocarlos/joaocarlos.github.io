## Project Overview

This extension program project aims to stimulate the interest in exact sciences through Robot Soccer.

## Objectives

1. Promote robot soccer in the region
2. Stimulate interest in exact sciences
3. Engage community in STEM activities
4. Train students in robotics

## Activities

-   Organization of the I Robotics Seminar of UFRB
