## Project Overview

The objective of this project is to carry out a study of the main techniques employed in the robot soccer project, with a view to developing a robot soccer team to work in the categories RoboCup Soccer Simulation 2D and Very Small Size.

## Objectives

1. Study and implement robot soccer techniques
2. Develop autonomous decision-making algorithms
3. Compete in RoboCup categories (Simulation 2D and VSS)
4. Train students in robotics and AI
