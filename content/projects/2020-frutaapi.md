## Project Overview

The project proposes to organize pest information affecting the cultivation of pineapple, passion fruit, papaya, banana, citrus, coconut, and mango into standardized knowledge bases with common and specific characteristics of each crop, to present to producers in a practical and focused way on the main problems faced.

## Objectives

1. Build standardized knowledge bases for tropical fruit pests
2. Develop digital platform for information access
3. Provide practical information for producers
4. Support pest management decisions

## Crops Covered

-   Pineapple
-   Passion fruit
-   Papaya
-   Banana
-   Citrus
-   Coconut
-   Mango
