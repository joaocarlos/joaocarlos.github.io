## Project Overview

This extension-program project aims to increase the interest in STEM through teaching microcontrolled systems (programming and digital circuits), and by using online platforms for Arduino development.

## Objectives

1. Teach microcontroller concepts
2. Introduce programming and digital circuits
3. Use online platforms for Arduino development
4. Stimulate interest in STEM fields
