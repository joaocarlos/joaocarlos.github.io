## Project Overview

The project aims to develop a robot colony based on artificial intelligence algorithms and multi-sensing systems to perform search, recognition, and inspection in an unstructured environment.

## Objectives

1. Develop multi-robot coordination algorithms
2. Implement autonomous navigation systems
3. Design multi-sensor fusion for environment perception
4. Test in unstructured environments
