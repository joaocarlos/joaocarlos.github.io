## Project Overview

The objective of this project is to stimulate interest in exact sciences and engineering through robot soccer.

## Objectives

1. Teach robot soccer concepts to the community
2. Stimulate interest in STEM fields
3. Develop educational materials for simulation platforms
4. Engage students from Recôncavo da Bahia region

## Activities

-   Workshop of Very Small Size Simulation (VSSim) category of RoboCup Soccer Simulation

## Impact

-   This project involved around 50 students from various high schools in the Recôncavo da Bahia region, promoting STEM education and interest in robotics through simulated robot soccer activities.
